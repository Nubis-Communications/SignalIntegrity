25  """TLineFourPortLossless
26  Ideal Lossless Four-port Transmission Line
27  @param Zc float or complex characteristic impedance
28  @param Td float electrical length (or time delay through the device)
29  @param f float frequency
30  @param Z0 (optional) float or complex characteristic impedance (defaults to 50 ohms)
31  @return list of list s-parameters of four-port lossless transmission line
32  @remark
33  This is actually an oddball construction and should not be confused with a typical differential
34  transmission line.\n
35  The symbol looks like a two-port transmission line with it's outer conductor exposed as ports on each
36  side.\n
37  Port 1 is the left side and port 2 is the right side of the two-port transmission line.\n
38  Port 3 is the left, exposed, outer conductor and port 4 is the right, exposed outer conductor.\n
39  @note this device is functionally equivalent to the two-port ideal lossless transmission line TLineFourPort() when
40  ports 3 and 4 are grounded.
41  """
42  return TLineFourPort(Zc,1j*2.*math.pi*f*Td,Z0)

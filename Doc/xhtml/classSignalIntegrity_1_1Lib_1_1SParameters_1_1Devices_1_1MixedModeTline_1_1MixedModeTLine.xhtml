Constructor The port numbering is ports 1 and 2 are the plus and minus terminals on one side of the line and ports 3 and 4 are the plus and minus terminals on the other side of the line.

Parameters
flist of float frequencies
Zdfloat or complex differential mode impedance
Tdfloat differential (or odd-mode) electrical length (the differential-mode propagation time.
Zcfloat or complex common-mode impedance
Tcfloat common (or even-mode) electrical length (the common-mode propagation time.
Z0(optional) float or complex reference impedance (defaults to 50 ohms).
Note
The differential mode impedance is twice the odd-mode impedance.
The common-mode impedance is half the even-mode impedance.
The model is appropriate for a balanced transmission line.
The s-parameters provided are single-ended.
This device builds a model internally and only solves the model on each access to getitem().

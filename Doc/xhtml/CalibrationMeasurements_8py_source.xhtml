24  """Constructor

28  The name of the measurement is not used for anything, but can be used to identify

30 
31  valid types of calibration measurements are:
32 
33  - 'reflect' - a reflect measurement taken on a port with a reflect standard
34  (like short, open, or load).
35  - 'thru' - a thru calibration measurement taken between two ports.
36  - 'xtalk' - a crosstalk calibration measurement typically taken between two ports that
37  are completely unconnected.
38  @see ReflectCalibrationMeasurement
39  @see ThruCalibrationMeasurement
40  @see XtalkCalibrationMeasurement
41  """
42  self.type=type
43  self.name=name

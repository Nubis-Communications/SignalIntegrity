49 def ShuntZTwoPort(Z,Z0=50.):
50  """ShuntZTwoPort
51  Two port shunt impedance
52  @param Z real or complex impedance of two-port impedance.
53  @param Z0 (optional) real or complex reference impedance (defaults to 50 ohms).
54  @return list of list s-parameter matrix of a shunt impedance.\n
55  @remark Ports 1 and 2 are connected together and to one side of the impedance.\n
56  The other side of the impedance is tied to ground.
57  """
58  D=(Z0+2.*Z)

26  """Converts power-wave s-parameters to pseudo-wave s-parameters
27  @param Sp list of list power-wave based s-parameter matrix to convert
28  @param Z0w (optional) the reference impedance of the pseudo-wave based s-parameters (assumed 50 ohms)
29  @param Z0p (optional) the reference impedance of the power-wave based s-parameters (assumed 50 ohms)
30  @param Kw (optional) the scaling factor of the pseudo-wave based s-parameters (assumed to be sqrt(Z0w)
31  @return the converted s-parameters in pseudo-waves
32  @see Z0KHelper to see how the reference impedance
33  and scaling factor are determined for pseudo-waves.
34  @see Z0KHelperPW to see the reference impedance
35  and scaling factor are determined for power waves.
36  """
37  if Z0p is None:
38  Z0p=Z0w
39  (Z0w,Kw)=Z0KHelper((Z0w,Kw),len(Sp))
40  (Z0p,Kp)=Z0KHelperPW(Z0p,len(Sp))
41  Sp=array(Sp)
42  Sw=inv(Kp).dot(Kw).dot(inv(Z0w)).dot((Z0w-Z0p.conj())+(Z0w+Z0p.conj()).dot(
43  Sp)).dot(inv((Z0w+Z0p)+(Z0w-Z0p).dot(Sp)).dot(Kp).dot(inv(Kw)).dot(Z0w))
44  return Sw.tolist()

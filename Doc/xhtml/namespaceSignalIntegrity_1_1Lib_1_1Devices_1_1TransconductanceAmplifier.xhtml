TransconductanceAmplifierFourPort Four port transconductance amplifier.

Parameters
Gfloat transconductance gain
Zifloat or complex input impedance
Zofloat or complex output impedance
Z0(optional) float reference impedance (defaults to 50 ohms)
Returns
list of list s-parameter matrix for a transconductance amplifier
Remarks
The voltage sense element senses the voltage with the plus terminal at port 1 and and the minus terminal at port 2 shunted with impedance of Zi.
The current generated by the amplifier enters port 4 and exits port 3 and is shunted with Zo.

TransconductanceAmplifierThreePort Three port transconductance amplifier.

Parameters
Gfloat transconductance gain
Zifloat or complex input impedance
Zofloat or complex output impedance
Z0(optional) float reference impedance (defaults to 50 ohms)
Returns
list of list s-parameter matrix for a transconductance amplifier.
Remarks
The three port transconductance amplifier is the same as the four port transconductance amplifier with ports two and three connected together and exposed as a single port.
The voltage sense element senses the voltage with the plus terminal at port 1 and and the minus terminal at port 3 shunted with impedance of Zi.
The current generated by the amplifier enters port 3 and exits port 2 and is shunted with Zo.

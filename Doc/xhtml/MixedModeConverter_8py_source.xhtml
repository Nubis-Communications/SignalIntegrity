25  """MixedModeConverterVoltage
26  Voltage mixed-mode converter
27  @return the s-parameter matrix of a voltage mixed-mode converter.
28  Ports 1 2 3 4 are + - D C
29 
30  this one has the right definition for differential
31  and common mode voltage the way we usually understand it meaning
32  that the differential voltage is the difference and the common-mode
33  voltage is the average of the plus and minus inputs.
34  """
35  DF=1.; CF=2.
36  return [[0.,0.,DF/2.,CF/2.],
37  [0.,0.,-DF/2.,CF/2.],
38  [1./DF,-1./DF,0.,0.],

24  """MixedModeConverterVoltage
25  Voltage mixed-mode converter
26  @return the s-paramater matrix of a voltage mixed-mode converter.
27  Ports 1 2 3 4 are + - D C
28 
29  this one has the right definition for differential
30  and common mode voltage the way we usually understand it meaning
31  that the differential voltage is the difference and the common-mode
32  voltage is the average of the plus and minus inputs.
33  """
34  DF=1.; CF=2.
35  return [[0.,0.,DF/2.,CF/2.],
36  [0.,0.,-DF/2.,CF/2.],
37  [1./DF,-1./DF,0.,0.],

28  constructs a waveform with a single pulse.
29  @param td instance of class TimeDescriptor containing time axis of waveform.
30  @param Amplitude (optional) float amplitude of pulse (defaults to unity).
31  @param StartTime (optional) float starting time of the pulse (defaults to zero).
32  @param PulseWidth (optional) float the width of the pulse (defaults to zero).
33  @param Risetime (optional) float risetime in seconds (defaults to 0.)
34  @note The amplitude can be positive or negative, with negative providing a negative

36  @note if the pulse appears entirely within the samples, then the waveform will be all zero.
37  @note actual risetime used is the sample period if less than that is specified
38  @note risetime is applied on the rising and falling edge. risetimes greater than

40  @note the risetime is applied such that the pulse reaches half amplitude at the start
41  time specified. Please note the expected non-causality.
42  """
